Why one mark can’t do every size
Same art, shrunk: the full 5-ring rosette dissolves into a smudge below ~48px. The fix is an optically-tiered system — fewer rings + heavier stroke as the surface shrinks — so the mark stays crisp on the tab bar, favicon and Android monochrome icon.
